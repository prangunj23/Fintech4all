    Frequently Asked Questions

    We highly encourage you to familiarize yourself with our FAQ section, so you can get answers to common inquiries regarding FinTech4All quickly

    When and where will the seminars usually take place?
    We will usually host our seminars at the New Castle County Libraries, specifically the Bear Library. The seminars will usually take place during the library hours of 10AM - 8PM.
    Who are the people behind FinTech4All?
    FinTech4All is run by Pranit Gunjal and Vishnu Gadicharla. We are passionate about the fields of finance and technology and want to share our passion with our guests.
    What prior knowledge will I need in order to attend the seminar?
    You will not need any prior knowledge before attending the seminar. Just bring yourself and be ready to learn.
