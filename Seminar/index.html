    Seminars

    Our Seminars give attendees critical information about the growing fields of Finance and Technology. We discuss topics such as loans, securities, and other financial properties. We also discuss programming and machine learning that is used in high-level trading. If you are interested in attending, please let us know.


    We partnered with the Delaware Community Reinvestment Action Council (DCRAC) and the Teen Warehouse to host our seminars. We have also partnered with BNY Mellon and gave a seminar to their KYC-AML team regarding crypto and securities.



    FinTech4All is a non-profit that promotes financial and technological literacy in our community. We teach people about how to improve their personal finance skills in order to lead better lives.
